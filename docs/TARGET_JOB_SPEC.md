# Target Job Spec: Senior Business Intelligence Developer

## Job Summary

We are seeking a highly experienced and results-driven Senior Business Intelligence Developer to lead the design, development, and implementation of advanced BI and AI-driven data solutions. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in data engineering, analytics, and business intelligence, with deep expertise in Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, and modern data platforms. You will play a key role in optimizing data integration, analytics, and reporting to drive business performance and innovation across global projects.

## Key Responsibilities

- Architect, develop, and optimize end-to-end data pipelines and data models using Microsoft Fabric, SQL Server, Azure Data Factory, and related technologies.
- Design and implement scalable data warehousing and data lake solutions for advanced analytics and reporting.
- Develop dynamic Power BI dashboards and reports, translating complex data into actionable business insights.
- Lead the development and deployment of AI-driven BI solutions, including intelligent data agents and automation tools.
- Oversee data governance, security, and compliance, including PII tracking and best practices.
- Administer and optimize Azure and Power BI environments, ensuring high performance, security, and cost efficiency.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, deliver tailored BI solutions, and provide strategic consulting.
- Conduct training sessions, workshops, and knowledge-sharing initiatives to elevate BI expertise within the organization.
- Mentor junior team members and contribute to a culture of continuous improvement and innovation.

## Required Skills and Experience

- Extensive experience in business intelligence, data engineering, and analytics, with a focus on Microsoft Fabric, Azure Data Factory, and Power BI.
- Advanced proficiency in SQL, DAX, Python, and R for data modeling, analytics, and automation.
- Proven ability to design and optimize database architectures for performance and scalability, both on-premises and cloud.
- Strong expertise in the Microsoft Power Platform: Power BI, Power Automate, and Power Apps.
- Demonstrated experience developing AI-driven data agents and automation solutions.
- Deep understanding of ETL/ELT processes, data pipelines, and orchestration.
- Experience with data governance, security, and compliance protocols.
- Excellent communication, training, and stakeholder management skills.
- International project experience and ability to deliver tailored solutions for diverse business needs.

## Qualifications and Preferred Background

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Data Science, or related field, or equivalent experience.
- Relevant certifications such as MSCA, Microsoft Power BI, or Azure Data Engineer are highly desirable.
- 8+ years of experience in BI, data engineering, or analytics roles.
- Preferred: market research, business development, leadership, finance, sales, HR, or geoscientific domain experience.
